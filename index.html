📄 FRD Weather Policy

Indoor Practice Severe Weather Policy – FRD (Advanced)

  • Tornado Warning: Cancel practice immediately and notify all members
  • Severe Thunderstorm Warning: Cancel practice immediately; assess venue safety for hail or wind damage
  • Winter Storm Warning: Cancel practice due to potential road hazards
  • Ice Storm Warning: Cancel practice if freezing rain or ice accumulation on roads reaches unsafe levels
  • Flood Warning: Cancel if travel routes to venue are flooded or impassable
  • Snow Accumulation ≥ 2" in 24 hours: Cancel practice due to poor road conditions
  • Ice Accumulation ≥ 0.25": Cancel practice if ice on roads or parking lot ≥ 0.25"
  • Rain Rate ≥ 0.5 in/hr: Cancel if sustained heavy rain causes flooding risk around venue
  • Wind Speed ≥ 40 mph (gusts ≥ 55 mph): Cancel practice if sustained wind threatens building safety
  • Hail Warning (Severe Thunderstorm Warning): Cancel practice if hail of any size is forecast or reported near venue
  • Visibility ≤ 0.25 miles: Cancel practice if visibility ≤ 0.25 miles due to heavy fog or blizzard conditions
  • Temperature ≤ 0 °F (with wind chill): Issue travel advisory; consider cancellation
  • Temperature ≥ 95 °F: Advise hydration and consider shortening practice if venue is not air-conditioned
  • Heat Index ≥ 100 °F: Advise hydration, schedule extra breaks, or cancel if extreme heat
  • AQI ≥ 100 (Unhealthy for Sensitive Groups): Recommend rescheduling if ventilation is poor
  • AQI ≥ 150 (Unhealthy): Cancel or move practice if ventilation is inadequate

📘 Practice Cancellation SOP

Standard Operating Procedure (SOP): Practice Cancellation Due to Weather

Applies to: Frederick Roller Derby

Practice Days: Mondays at 6:30 PM and Fridays at 7:30 PM

🔍 Purpose

To ensure the safety of skaters and volunteers by providing a consistent, timely, and responsible process for cancelling practice due to severe weather or unsafe travel conditions.

📅 Schedule for Review

  • Initial Check: By 12:00 PM on practice day
  • Final Decision Deadline:
    • Monday: no later than 4:00 PM
    • Friday: no later than 5:00 PM

🌤️ Evaluation Criteria

  • Active Tornado Warnings
  • Severe Thunderstorm Warnings (hail/wind risk)
  • Winter Storm, Ice Storm, or Flood Warnings
  • Snow ≥ 2" in 24 hours
  • Ice ≥ 0.25"
  • Rain Rate ≥ 0.5 in/hr
  • Wind ≥ 40 mph (gusts ≥ 55 mph)
  • Hail forecast or reports
  • Visibility ≤ 0.25 miles
  • Temperature ≤ 0 °F or ≥ 95 °F
  • Heat Index ≥ 100 °F
  • AQI ≥ 100 (Unhealthy for Sensitive Groups) or ≥ 150 (Unhealthy)

👤 Decision-Makers

The Head Coach makes the decision. If unavailable, a Board Member (President or VP) will step in. All decisions should be shared in the board Slack thread.

📣 Communication of Cancellation

  • Deadline to Announce:
    • Monday: by 4:00 PM
    • Friday: by 5:00 PM
  • Announcer: Head Coach or assigned Board Member
  • Channels:
    • #practice-attendance and #announcements Slack channels

📢 Sample Message

🚨 Practice Cancelled Tonight 🚨
Due to [brief reason], we are cancelling tonight’s practice (DATE).
Stay safe — we’ll see you next time!
— FRD Leadership
